The Solution

How SAR-G Works

A fully autonomous cycle — from deployment to treatment report — with zero human entry into the silo.

01
Swims
SAR-G is deployed from the silo top hatch. Using counter-rotating augers, it swims downward through the grain mass — reaching depths of 10m+ without cables.
AUGER PROPULSION · 10M DEPTH · CABLE-FREE · GPS-DENIED NAV
02
Detects
Onboard sensors continuously measure CO₂, relative humidity, and temperature. The probe builds a real-time 3D heatmap of spoilage zones invisible from the surface.
CO₂ NDIR SENSOR · RH/T · 3D VOLUMETRIC MAPPING · REAL-TIME
03
Responds
Upon detecting a spoilage hotspot (CO₂ > 2000 ppm), SAR-G activates precision phosphine micro-fumigation — treating only the affected zone, not the entire silo.
PHOSPHINE MICRO-DOSE · PRECISION TARGETING · 90% CHEMICAL REDUCTION
04
Reports
All data is transmitted wirelessly to a cloud dashboard. Silo operators receive zone-level reports, treatment logs, and predictive spoilage risk scores.
WIRELESS UPLINK · CLOUD DASHBOARD · PREDICTIVE ANALYTICS · API
